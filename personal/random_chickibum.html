Research in Multilevel Diffractive Optics
It is so sad to see that despite efforts being undertaken to revive the field of multilevel diffractive optics, a lot of industrial and academic research funding is going towards meta-optics. I believe people (investors and funding agencies) need to understand that 1 or 2 research groups working in multilevel diffractive optics can never produce the same output compared to ~50+ meta-optics groups. Neither putting "extra" money into those 1 or 2 research groups will also help. One will need to trust the out-going Ph.D. students from those groups, give them opportunitites and fund their research to expand the area and it is only then can you expect good research in multilevel diffractive optics. I don't realize what kind of (government as well as industrial) adminstration is this! Even a fool can understand this.... Research nowadays is a cumulative effort. It is not a "one-man" show.
